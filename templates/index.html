Hashnotes

What is this?

Effortlessly create and share Markdown notes—no login required. Whether you’re jotting down ideas, writing code snippets, or crafting longer content for platforms like Twitter or Bluesky, Hashnotes makes it easy to share text online. Just write, share, and go. No accounts, no hassle.

How do I use it?

Just hit the New button to start creating a new note. When you're done writing, hit "Submit" and you'll be taken to your note, which you can share with the rest of the world. If you're not happy with the result, hit "Edit" and draft a new version of the note.

Important Note

Please be aware: Notes cannot currently be edited or deleted after publishing. Make sure to double-check your content before sharing. Think twice, post once!
